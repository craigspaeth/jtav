About the procedure

These peels, designed specifically for your skin type, will work by revealing healthy and glowing skin. Peels allow dead skin to slough off, leaving behind a layer of smooth, softer skin. Peels work to unclog pores, treat acne, decrease sun damage, fine lines, and wrinkles and pigment irregularities.

SkinMedica Illuminize Peel: for mild skin imperfections

SkinMedica Vitalize Peel: for mild to moderate skin imperfections

SkinMedica Rejuvenize Peel: for moderate to severe skin imperfections
