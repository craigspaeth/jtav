Procedures / JTav Anti- aging Facial

About the procedure

Joie Tavernise specially designed this facial to help prevent, treat, and protect the dermis from signs of aging. The treatment begins with a light exfoliation to remove superficial impurities and aid in cell turnover, leaving your skin with a luminous glow. The treatment will be completed with a luxurious LED photo light therapy that eliminates bacteria build up, stimulates collagen growth, and promotes healing.
